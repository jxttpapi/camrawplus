Privacy Policy

How CamRAW+ handles your data — and why there's almost nothing to say.

The short version: CamRAW+ collects no personal data, uses no analytics, and requires no account. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

CamRAW+ is a professional RAW camera app for iPhone. This policy explains what the app accesses, why, and what happens to that data.

CamRAW+ is designed from the ground up to work entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

Device permissions

📷
Camera — Required
Used to show the live viewfinder and capture photos. Camera data is processed locally and never transmitted off your device.
🖼️
Photo Library (write only) — Required
Used to save captured RAW and DNG images to your Camera Roll. CamRAW+ does not read your existing photo library.

What we don't collect

CamRAW+ does not collect, transmit, store, or share any of the following:

Personal information
Photos or images
Location data
Device identifiers
Usage analytics
Crash reports
Advertising IDs
Any tracking data

Third-party services

CamRAW+ does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. No data is shared with any third party under any circumstances.

Children's privacy

CamRAW+ does not collect any information from any user, including children under 13. The app fully complies with COPPA and similar regulations because no data collection occurs at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the effective date below will change. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updates.
